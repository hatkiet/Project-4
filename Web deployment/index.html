Heart Attack Project

Why Heart Attack?

Heart attacks are a leading cause of morbidity and mortality worldwide. Understanding the distribution and risk factors of heart attacks is crucial for improving public health outcomes. By analyzing data on heart attacks across different regions and demographics, we can identify patterns and potential areas for intervention. This webpage aims to provide insights into the prevalence of heart attacks in the USA and explore various factors that may influence the likelihood of experiencing a heart attack. Learn More!
